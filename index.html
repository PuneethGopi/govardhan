Slider

Double slider windows create more air circulation since both sashes open and close by gliding to the left or right. Since double sliders do not open outward, they are ideal for rooms near shrubs, trees, walkways, and patios.

Casement Window

Side hung windows are a highly cost effective option and one of our most popular window types. They are hinged on the side and open towards the outside.

Top Hung

Top hung windows are a highly popular type of window and our most cost effective option. These windows can be supplied with single glazing or double glazing, depending on the energy efficiency requirements of your project. They are hinged at the top and open towards the outside.

Patio Door

A sliding glass door, patio door, or doorwall is a type of sliding door in architecture and construction, is a large glass window opening in a structure that provide door access from a room to the outdoors, fresh air, and copious natural light.

Swing Door

Double doors are our most popular doors. They are perfect for places where you need a little more space – like access to a patio. uPVC Double doors can be fitted to a parliament-style hinge , allowing them to open up a full 180 degrees. They are hinged on the sides. Both sides of the door open, allowing for a big opening.

Fixed/Picture window

Sliding doors are one of our most cost effective doors. They can accommodate very big openings where you do not want to have a door in the way.

FEATURES

Energy Efficient

uPVC windows and doors are energy efficient. uPVC framed windows and doors can keep you cool by reducing solar heat gain from outside.

All Weather

uPVC frames are a very good thermal insulator which can withstand any weather and dramatically reduce the possibility of condensation on window frames.

Low Maintenance

Unlike timber framed that require regular maintenance, uPVC framed windows and doors only need a little maintenance.

Watertight

uPVC windows and doors profile are fusion welded at corners. There is no gap for any water getting through into the building structure.

Noise Reduction

Double glazing is one of the most effective barriers against sound, and combined with uPVC window frame systems can reduce perceived noise by up to 80%.

Long Lasting

Our uPVC profiles are formulated for high UV weather conditions and have an expected service life of over 40 years.
